[1015] Dynamic Currency Conversion (DCC)
[1016] DCC processing fee of 3.5% will be charged on the billed amount with effect from 15th January 2022 for both Credit and Debit Card transactions.
[1017] This fee will be shown as a separate transaction and processed to the Credit Card/Bank Account within 7 working days from the transaction posting date.
[1018] The stamp duty of 2.5% will be applicable for DCC Credit Card transactions.
[1019] What is a DCC transaction?
[1020] A DCC transaction is a service offered at POS, E-commerce or any card acceptance modes by merchants/service providers who are located locally or overseas or online. The DCC service converts a foreign currency transaction into the currency of the Credit Card/Bank Account at the point of sale and processes the agreed billing currency
[1021] amount to the Credit Card/Bank Account.
[1022] What happens if the transaction is converted via DCC service?
[1023] The merchant/service provider will convert the foreign currency transaction amount into the currency of the Credit Card/Bank Account using an exchange rate determined by the relevant merchant/service provider and agreed with the cardholder. This rate may di er from the foreign exchange rates being used by Visa, Mastercard or other
[1024] relevant card scheme associations.
